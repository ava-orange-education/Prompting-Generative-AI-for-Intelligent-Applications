General Company FAQ

What are your business hours?
Our customer support team is available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time. On weekends, we offer limited support from 10 AM to 2 PM Eastern Time. During public holidays, our office is closed but you can still reach us by email.

How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our support team through the following channels. Email us at support@example.com for general inquiries. Call us at 1-800-555-0199 for urgent matters. Use the live chat feature on our website for quick questions during business hours.

What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in its original packaging and unused condition. To start a return, log into your account and go to Order History. Select the item you wish to return and follow the instructions. A return shipping label will be emailed to you within 24 hours. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after we receive the returned item.

How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also check your order status by logging into your account and visiting the Order History section. Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to appear after shipping.

Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on the destination and package weight. Delivery times for international orders typically range from 7 to 14 business days. Please note that customs fees and import taxes are the responsibility of the buyer.

Can I cancel my order after placing it?
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that window, the order may have already been processed for shipping. To cancel, go to your Order History and click Cancel Order. If the option is no longer available, please contact support and we will do our best to help.

Do you have a loyalty program?
Yes, we offer a rewards program for returning customers. You earn 1 point for every dollar spent. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases. Sign up for free through your account settings under the Rewards tab.
