HR Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the Company

Congratulations on joining the team. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know during your first few weeks. Our goal is to help you feel comfortable, informed, and ready to contribute.

Before Your First Day
Your offer letter and employment contract will be sent to your email within 3 business days of accepting the offer. Please sign and return them before your start date. You will also receive login credentials for your company email and the HR portal. If you do not receive these, contact hr@example.com.

First Day Checklist
On your first day, please arrive at the office by 9:30 AM. Visit the reception desk and ask for your onboarding coordinator. You will receive your employee ID badge, laptop, and welcome kit. The IT team will help you set up your devices and install required software. Your manager will introduce you to your team during the morning.

First Week Activities
During your first week, you will attend orientation sessions covering company values, team structure, and key policies. You will also complete mandatory compliance training on data security and workplace safety. Your manager will schedule a one-on-one meeting to discuss your role, responsibilities, and goals for the first 90 days.

Probation Period
All new employees go through a 3-month probation period. During this time, your manager will provide regular feedback on your performance. A formal review will take place at the end of the probation period. Successful completion leads to confirmation of your permanent role.

Key Contacts
For HR-related questions, contact the HR team at hr@example.com. For IT support, use the helpdesk portal or email it-support@example.com. For facilities and office supplies, contact facilities@example.com. Your onboarding coordinator will be your primary point of contact during the first month.

Benefits Enrollment
You are eligible for company benefits from your start date. These include health insurance, dental coverage, and a retirement savings plan. You must enroll within 30 days of joining. Details and enrollment forms are available on the HR portal under the Benefits section.

Learning and Development
The company offers a learning budget of 1,500 dollars per year for each employee. You can use this for courses, certifications, conferences, or books related to your role. Requests are submitted through the HR portal and require manager approval. Unused learning budget does not carry forward to the next year.
