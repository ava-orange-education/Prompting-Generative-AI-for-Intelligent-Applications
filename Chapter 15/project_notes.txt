Project Name: Internal Leave Management System
Purpose:
This system helps employees apply for leave and allows managers to approve or reject requests.
Key Features:
•	Employees submit leave requests online.
•	Managers review and approve requests.
•	HR can view leave history and generate reports.
Common Issues:
•	Users sometimes forget to upload supporting documents.
•	Approval delays occur during peak holiday seasons.
Planned Improvements:
•	Add email notifications for approvals.
•	Create a mobile friendly interface.
