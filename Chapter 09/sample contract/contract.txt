Sample Contract Agreement

Contract Title: Service Agreement
Effective Date: 10 January 2025

Parties:
This agreement is made between:

Client: The individual or organization requesting services.

Service Provider: The individual or organization delivering the services.

1. Scope of Work

The Service Provider agrees to perform software development, bug fixes, and maintenance tasks as requested by the Client. All deliverables will be submitted in digital format.

2. Payment Terms

The Client agrees to pay a monthly fee of INR 50,000 for the services. Payment is due within 10 days of receiving the invoice.

3. Timeline

The Service Provider will begin work on the Effective Date and continue on a monthly basis until the agreement is terminated by either party.

4. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep all project data, documents, and communication confidential. No information may be shared without written consent.

5. Ownership

All code, documents, and deliverables created under this agreement will be the property of the Client once full payment is made.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 15 days written notice. All pending payments must be settled before termination becomes effective.

7. Liability

The Service Provider will not be responsible for indirect or consequential damages. Liability is limited to the amount paid in the last 30 days.

8. Signatures

Client Signature: _______________________
Service Provider Signature: _______________________
Date: _______________________
